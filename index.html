About Us

Safari Computer Club, formerly known as "Computing Systems and Hackathons", is a community that was formed to foster knowledge sharing, networking and collaborative projects. The approach was combining students from different universities in Kenya, industry experts, engineers, Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors, Google WTM Ambassadors, Huawei Student Developers, and graduates currently in the field. Overtime this has become a gateway to bridging students to the industry and upskilling members. Join us in this journey full of learning and fun as we explore our true capabilities combined!
